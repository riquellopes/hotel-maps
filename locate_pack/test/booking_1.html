Best Western Plus Sol Ipanema offers a superb location on the Ipanema Beach seafront, within a vibrant area of bars and restaurants. It features a sunny terrace with breathtaking coast views. Wi-Fi is free.

Best Western Plus Sol Ipanema Hotel offers spacious air-conditioned rooms with a bright contemporary décor. All feature a 32-inch LCD TV and minibar, plus a luxurious bathroom with amenities and a magnifying mirror. Some rooms boast scenic views of Rio de Janeiro’s most famous beachfront.

Buffet breakfast is served at the hotel´s restaurant and includes a rich selection of tropical fruits, breads and cakes. Guests can admire panoramic sea views while enjoying their meals or having drinks on the terrace, by the pool.

The hotel is located just 1 block away from the historic Garota de Ipanema Bar. Rio de Janeiro’s city centre and Santos Dumont Airport are just 13 km away. The Leblon shopping area can be reached within a 10-minute walk.

The 24-hour front desk can arrange car/bicycle rental and shuttle services. Beauty shop treatments and child services can also be arranged. Third-party parking lots can be found nearby the hotel.
